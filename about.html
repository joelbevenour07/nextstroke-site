About Us

Luke Iannelli is a lightweight rower and the founder of NextStroke, known for his competitive mindset and disciplined approach to training.

After not completing his freshman year, Luke fully bought into the behind the scenes details that make rowers fast. His commitment paid off as he became a decorated athlete at La Salle. He spent time in the 1v ranked #15 in the nation, won three Head of the Charles medals, qualified and raced at Henley, earned a PAC-12 qualification, and more. All while raising the standard for his team.

Luke created NextStroke to pass on the lessons, systems, and details so other rowers can shorten the learning curve and raise their own standard.

Joel Bevenour

Joel Bevenour was a heavyweight rower for La Salle University, and acted as their captain for 3 years in a row.

Ever since he stepped onto campus, he had a drive to lead by example and put his best foot forward every day. Joel utilized the wisdom he learned from his talented coaching staff and teammates to demonstrate what the model athlete should do; day in and day out. He was a part of the currently #15 ranked 1V boat in the nation for most of his Sophomore to Senior year, medaled at regattas such as Head of the Charles, raced at the prestigious Henley Royal Regatta, and voted 2025 IRCA's Comeback Athlete of the Year.

Joel wants to utilize his lived experience to help athletes on the road to success.
